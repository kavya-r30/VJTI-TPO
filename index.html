About Us

We, at VJTI Mumbai, are committed to providing excellent training and placement opportunities to our students. Our Training and Placement Office (TPO) plays a pivotal role in connecting students with Industry professionals, Start-ups, R&D organization, Govt. organization and facilitating their career growth. We focus not only on academic excellence but also on developing well-rounded individuals who are ready to embark on successful professional journeys.

Placements

Our TPO collaborates closely with leading companies across various industries to bring exciting placement opportunities to our students. We organize campus interviews, career fairs, and workshops to help students explore their career options and boost their employability skills. Our alumni network also plays an active role in connecting students with job openings and offering mentorship.

Our Recruiters

We have strong partnerships with a wide range of recruiters from various industries. Our TPO works closely with these recruiters to understand their hiring needs and connect them with our talented students. We are proud to have recruiters who consistently hire our students and provide them with rewarding career opportunities. Some of our esteemed recruiters include: Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Tata Group, Infosys.

Past Placements

Our students have been successfully placed in top companies across various industries. Some of the prominent companies where our students have secured placements include: Google, Microsoft, Amazon, TCS. We are proud of our students' achievements and their contribution to these renowned organizations.
